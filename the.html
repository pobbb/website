Who is Mark Edward Fischbach.

Mark Edward Fischbach also known as Markiplier, is a Youtuber with 29.9 million
subscribers. He has 16,466,836,154 as of September
16th, 2021. He is most well known for his Let's Plays and Walkthroughs of video games.
He was born in Honolulu, Hawaii. He was 10lbs 3oz when he was born.
He grew up in Cincinnati, Ohio. During his childhood he played on computer alot but
also played outside with his brother alot. When he first was going into college his
father developed cancer. The next year his father passed away from cancer. He then
went through college to study biomedical enginnering at the University of Cincinnati,
However he dropped out to pursue his Youtube career.

His career in youtube.

Markiplier started off his youtube career doing let's plays of horror games, YouTube then
banned his AdSense account. He then created a new channel by the name of "markiplierGame"
Markiplier then moved to Los Angeles to get closer to resources used to create his videos.
In August 2014, Markiplier began his playthrough of Five Night's at Freddy, Which was his most popular series to date.
Him and some of his friends took a break from YouTube for a little while and shortly after they came back
and started uploading videos again, on October 15th 2015, he hit 10 million subscribers.
That same year he co-hosted the 2015 South by Southwest Gaming Awards. A few years later, on March 29th, 2018,
his channel passed 20 million subscribers. Now, Markiplier does let's plays of horror games
just like he used to and he also plays other games too.

Good things he has done.

Markiplier is known for activily doing charity streams, in which all funds donated to
the stream go towards a charity of his choice. From his charity streams he, his
friends, and his viewers have been able to raise thousands for multiple different
charitys. In 2013 he had 12 charity livestreams raising at least, $495,113.12.
In 2014-2016 he had 9 charity livestreams raising at least $563,591.
